STATUS REPORT BY CHINA
The following communication, dated 10 July 2014, from the delegation of China to the Chairperson of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated pursuant to Article 21.6 of the DSU.

Status Report Regarding Implementation of the DSB Recommendations and Rulings in the Dispute China ­ Anti-Dumping and Countervailing Duty Measures
on Broiler Products from the United States (WT/DS427)
China submits this report in accordance with Article 21.6 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes.
At its meeting on 25 September 2013, the Dispute Settlement Body ("DSB") adopted the panel report in the dispute China ­ Anti-Dumping and Countervailing Duty Measures on Broiler Products from the United States (WT/DS427). On 22 October 2013, China informed the DSB of its intention to implement the recommendations and rulings of the DSB in connection with this matter.
On 19 December 2013, China and the United States informed the DSB that they had agreed that the reasonable period of time for China to implement the recommendations and rulings of the DSB shall be 9 months and 14 days from the date of adoption of the panel report. Accordingly, the reasonable period of time expires on 9 July 2014.
On 25 December 2013, the Ministry of Commerce of China published a notice (Notice (2013) No. 88) and launched re-investigation on Broiler Products from the United States. On 8 July 2014, the Ministry of Commerce of China published a notice (Notice (2014) No. 44) and announced the determination of the re-investigation on Broiler Products from the United States.
Through the re-investigation mentioned above, China has fully implemented the DSB recommendations and rulings in this dispute.
