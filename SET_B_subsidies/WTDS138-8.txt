UNITED STATES ­ IMPOSITION OF COUNTERVAILING DUTIES ON CERTAIN HOT-ROLLED LEAD AND BISMUTH CARBON STEEL PRODUCTS ORIGINATING IN THE UNITED KINGDOM
Communication from the Appellate Body
The following communication, dated 10 May 2000, from Mr. Florentino Feliciano, Chairman of the Appellate Body, addressed to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 17.5 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes.
_______________
It is my honour to transmit to you the Report of the Appellate Body in United States ­ Imposition of Countervailing Duties on Certain Hot-Rolled Lead and Bismuth Carbon Steel Products Originating in the United Kingdom , WT/DS138/AB/R. It will be circulated later today, to Members of the World Trade Organization, in accordance with paragraph 5 of Article 17 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes. The three Members of the Appellate Body who served on this appeal were Mr. Mitsuo Matsushita, as Presiding Member; Mr. Said El-Naggar and Mr. Julio Lacarte-Muró, as Members.
