INDONESIA - CERTAIN MEASURES AFFECTING THE AUTOMOBILE INDUSTRY
Request to Join Consultations
Communication from Canada
The following communication, dated 24 October 1996, from the Permanent Mission of Canada to the Permanent Mission of Indonesia, the Permanent Mission of the United States and to the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 4.11 of the DSU.
_______________
Pursuant to paragraph 11 of Article 4 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes, the Government of Canada hereby notifies you that due to its substantial trade interest, it desires to be joined in the consultations under Article XXII of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade 1994 requested by the Permanent Mission of the United States to the WTO in Geneva by letter dated 8 October 1996, regarding certain measures affecting trade and investment in the motor vehicle sector implemented by Indonesia through Presidential Decrees 54/1993 and 42/1996, Presidential Instruction 2/1996, numerous ministerial decrees and government regulations, and other governmental measures (WT/DS59/1).
