UNITED STATES ­ DOMESTIC SUPPORT AND EXPORT CREDIT GUARANTEES FOR AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS
Request to Join Consultations
Communication from Costa Rica
The following communication, dated 24 July 2007, from the delegation of Costa Rica to the delegation of the United States, the delegation of Brazil and to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 4.11 of the DSU.
_______________
With regard to Brazil's request to the United States for consultations in United States ­ Domestic Support and Export Credit Guarantees for Agricultural Products, circulated on 17 July 2007 (WT/DS365/1), pursuant to Article 4.11 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes I hereby submit a request from the Government of Costa Rica to be joined in these consultations.
Costa Rica has a substantial trade interest in these consultations, and looks forward to a favourable reply to this request.
