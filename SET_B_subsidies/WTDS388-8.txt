CHINA ­ GRANTS, LOANS AND OTHER INCENTIVES
Request to Join Consultations
Communication from Guatemala
The following communication, dated 19 January 2009, from the delegation of Guatemala to the delegation of China, the delegation of Mexico and to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 4.11 of the DSU.
_______________
Pursuant to Article 4.11 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes (DSU), Guatemala hereby notifies the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government of Mexico, and the Dispute Settlement Body that, in the light of the substantial trade interest of Guatemala, it desires to be joined in the consultations requested by Mexico concerning China ­ Grants, Loans and Other Incentives (WT/DS388/1, circulated on 8 January 2009).
Guatemala has a substantial trade interest in the matters under consultation. Guatemala's industries are directly affected by the measures challenged by Mexico in its request for consultations. Guatemala also has an interest in the correct application of the WTO agreements relating to subsidies.
Guatemala would appreciate being informed of the date and venue of the consultations.
