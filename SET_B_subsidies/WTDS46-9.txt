BRAZIL - EXPORT FINANCING PROGRAMME FOR AIRCRAFT
Communication from the Appellate Body
The following communication, dated 2 August 1999, from Mr. Said El-Naggar, Chairman of the Appellate Body, addressed to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 17.5 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes.
_________________
It is my honour to transmit to you the Report of the Appellate Body in Brazil - Export Financing Programme for Aircraft, WT/DS46/AB/R. It will be circulated as an unrestricted document to Members of the World Trade Organization at 4 p.m. today, in accordance with paragraph 5 of Article 17 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes. The three Members of the Appellate Body who served on this appeal were myself, as Presiding Member; Mr. James Bacchus and Mr. Claus-Dieter Ehlermann, as Members.
