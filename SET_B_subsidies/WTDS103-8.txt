CANADA ­ MEASURES AFFECTING THE IMPORTATION OF MILK AND THE EXPORTATION OF DAIRY PRODUCTS
Communication from the Appellate Body
The following communication, dated 13 October 1999, from Mr. Said El-Naggar, Chairman of the Appellate Body, addressed to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 17.5 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes.
_______________
It is my honour to transmit to you the Report of the Appellate Body in Canada ­ Measures Affecting the Importation of Milk and the Exportation of Dairy Products, WT/DS103/AB/R, WT/DS113/AB/R. It will be circulated today to Members of the World Trade Organization, in accordance with paragraph 5 of Article 17 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes. The three Members of the Appellate Body who served on this appeal were Mr. Mitsuo Matsushita, as Presiding Member; Mr. Florentino Feliciano and Mr. Julio Lacarte-Muró, as Members.
