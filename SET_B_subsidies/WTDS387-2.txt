CHINA ­ GRANTS, LOANS AND OTHER INCENTIVES
Request to Join Consultations
Communication from Mexico
The following communication, dated 15 January 2009, from the delegation of Mexico to the delegation of China, the delegation of the United States and to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 4.11 of the DSU.
_______________
My authorities have instructed me to notify you that, pursuant to Article 4.11 of the WTO Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes, the Government of Mexico wishes to be joined in the consultations requested by the United States in a communication circulated on 7 January 2009 (WT/DS387/1) entitled China ­ Grants, Loans and Other Incentives. The Government of Mexico has a substantial trade interest in these measures, in connection with which it has already submitted a request for consultations in document WT/DS388/1.
