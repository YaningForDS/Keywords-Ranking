UNITED STATES ­ DOMESTIC SUPPORT AND EXPORT CREDIT GUARANTEES FOR AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS
Request to Join Consultations
Communication from India
The following communication, dated 26 July 2007, from the delegation of India to the delegation of the United States, the delegation of Brazil and to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 4.11 of the DSU.
_______________
This concerns the request for consultations by the Government of Brazil in United States ­ Domestic Support and Export Credit Guarantee for Agricultural Products, circulated on 17 July 2007 (WT/DS365/1). My authorities have instructed me to notify the consulting Members and the Dispute Settlement Body of the desire of the Government of India to be joined in these consultations, pursuant to Article 4.11 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes (DSU).
India has a substantial trade interest in these consultations, and looks forward to a favourable reply to this request.
