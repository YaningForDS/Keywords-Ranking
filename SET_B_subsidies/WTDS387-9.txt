CHINA ­ GRANTS, LOANS AND OTHER INCENTIVES
Request to Join Consultations
Communication from Ecuador
The following communication, dated 19 January 2009, from the delegation of Ecuador to the delegation of China, the delegation of the United States and to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 4.11 of the DSU.
_______________
Pursuant to Article 4.11 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes (DSU), Ecuador hereby notifies the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the United States, and the Dispute Settlement Body that, in the light of the substantial trade interest of Ecuador, it desires to be joined in the consultations requested by the United States concerning China ­ Grants, Loans and Other Incentives (WT/DS387/1, circulated on 7 January 2009).
Ecuador has a substantial trade interest in the matters under consultation. Ecuador's industries are directly affected by the measures challenged by the United States in its request for consultations. Ecuador also has an interest in the correct application of the WTO agreements relating to subsidies.
Ecuador would appreciate being informed of the date and venue of the consultations.
