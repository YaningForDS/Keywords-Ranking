INDONESIA ­ CERTAIN MEASURES AFFECTING THE AUTOMOBILE INDUSTRY
Arbitration under Article 21.3(c) of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes
The following communication, dated 4 December 1998, from the arbitrator, Mr. Christopher Beeby to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 21.3(c) of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes.
_____________________
It is my honour to transmit to you my Arbitration Award in Indonesia - Certain Measures Affecting the Automobile Industry, pursuant to Article 21.3(c) of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes. It will be circulated to the Members of the WTO in all three official languages on Monday, 7 December 1998, and it is a confidential document until that date.
