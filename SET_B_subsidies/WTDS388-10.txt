CHINA ­ GRANTS, LOANS AND OTHER INCENTIVES
Request to Join Consultations
Communication from New Zealand
The following communication, dated 19 January 2009, from the delegation of New Zealand to the delegation of China, the delegation of Mexico and to the Chairman of the Dispute Settlement Body, is circulated in accordance with Article 4.11 of the DSU.
_______________
Pursuant to paragraph 11 of Article 4 of the Understanding on Rules and Procedures Governing the Settlement of Disputes (DSU), the Government of New Zealand hereby notifies the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the Mexico and the Dispute Settlement Body that New Zealand desires to be joined in the consultations requested by Mexico in a communication circulated to WTO Members on 7 January 2009 (WT/DS388/1, G/L/880, G/SCM/D82/1, G/AG/GEN/80) and entitled China ­ Grants, Loans and Other Incentives.
New Zealand has a substantial trade interest in the subject of the consultations, including its relevant trade in agricultural products, and therefore desires to be joined in these consultations. New Zealand looks forward to confirmation of receipt and acceptance of this request to join the consultations and advice regarding the timing and venue for the consultations.
